Whoops! Online

Discard Pile

Rules

Whoops! Online is a game similar to Sorry! where players race their pieces around the board to get them "home."

  • Each player has 4 pieces.
  • Players draw cards to move their pieces.
  • To move a piece out of the start area, a player must draw a 1 or 2.
  • Pieces move according to the number on the drawn card.
  • Special Cards:
    • Whoops: Switch places with another player.
    • Wipe Out: Send any one piece back to its start area.
  • To win, all 4 pieces must reach the home area by exact count.
