Whoops! Online

Game Rules

Objective: Move all your pieces from the start to the "Home" area before your opponents do.

Setup:

  • Each player starts with 4 pieces in their start area.
  • The board consists of 16x16 squares with a designated start area, home area, and a path connecting them.

Card Play:

  • Players draw cards from the deck to determine their moves.
  • Some cards allow players to move forward, backward, or perform special actions.

Special Cards:

  • Whoops: Move one piece to the start.
  • Wipe Out: Remove an opponent's piece from the board.

Movement:

  • Players move pieces according to the card drawn.
  • Landing on a square occupied by an opponent sends their piece back to start.
